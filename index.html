Planeteer by Riley and Tim Hall

Planeteer is a game created by my brother and me over the course of this year in collaboration over the Internet. The game is based around realistic physics and planetary motion and was built from the ground-up, including physics and game engines.

The premise of the game is that your ship has run out of fuel and must reach the nearest refueling station (the spinning blue circle). You have the power to control planets, and by using the planets' gravitational pull you can guide your ship safely home.

  • Click and drag to move the planets.
  • The sun is too massive and cannot be moved.
  • Watch the predictions! Red means a probable failure; green means you found a safe path.
  • You can look around! Use the scroll wheel to zoom in and out; Shift+drag pans the camera.
  • There are many ways to the destination, try switching it up if you're having trouble.
  • You are able to move the destination, make your own challenges if you want to keep playing.
